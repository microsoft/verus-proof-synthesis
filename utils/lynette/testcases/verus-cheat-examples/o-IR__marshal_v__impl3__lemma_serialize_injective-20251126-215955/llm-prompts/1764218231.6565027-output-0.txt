Explanation of changes:
The compilation error arises from ambiguous integer literals inside seq! macros. Since ghost_serialize returns Seq<u8>, the literals inside seq! must be u8-typed. I replaced all occurrences of seq![0] and seq![1] with seq![0u8] and seq![1u8], respectively, in the Option implementation and associated proof assertions. This resolves the type inference errors without altering logic.
