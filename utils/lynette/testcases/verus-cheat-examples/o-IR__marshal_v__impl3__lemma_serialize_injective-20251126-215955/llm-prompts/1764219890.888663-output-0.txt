Explanation:
The compilation error arises from the invalid use of the Rust "as" cast on a reference: (o) as &T. In Verus, "as" is intended for numeric casts and leads to a trait error attempting to treat &T as an Integer. The variable o already has type &T, so no cast is needed. We fix this by directly passing o to view_equal.
