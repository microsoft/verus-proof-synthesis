Explanation of changes:
The compilation error arises from ambiguous integer literals in sequences intended to be sequences of bytes (Seq<u8>). The seq![0] and seq![1] literals need explicit u8 suffixes to resolve type inference. I updated:
- Option<T>::ghost_serialize to use seq![0u8] and seq![1u8].
- All related assertions and sequence expressions in the proof to use 0u8/1u8 consistently, including indexing and subrange contexts.

These changes disambiguate the literal types and align with the expected Seq<u8> type.
