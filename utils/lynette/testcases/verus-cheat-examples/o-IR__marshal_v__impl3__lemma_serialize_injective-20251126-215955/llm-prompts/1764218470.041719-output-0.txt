Explanation of changes:
The error arises from ambiguous integer literals in seq! macros used to build Seq<u8> values. Verus cannot infer that 0 and 1 are u8; thus we must explicitly annotate them as u8. I updated all seq![0] and seq![1] occurrences related to Seq<u8> to seq![0u8] and seq![1u8], including inside assertions and subrange expressions.
