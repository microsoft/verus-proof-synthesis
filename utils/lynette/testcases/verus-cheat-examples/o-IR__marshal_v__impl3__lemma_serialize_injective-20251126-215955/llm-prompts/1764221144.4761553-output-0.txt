Explanation:
The compilation error arises because seq![0] and seq![1] are ambiguous integer literals when constructing Seq<u8>. We must suffix these literals with u8. I updated:
- Option<T>::ghost_serialize to return seq![0u8] and seq![1u8].
- All occurrences in the proof of lemma_serialize_injective where seq![0] and seq![1] occur, including composite expressions like seq![1] + ... and index/select cases.

These changes disambiguate the types and adhere to Verus's integer literal requirements.
