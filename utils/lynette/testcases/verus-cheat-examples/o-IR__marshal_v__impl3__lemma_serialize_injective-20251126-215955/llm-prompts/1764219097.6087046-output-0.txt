Explanation:
The error arises because seq![0] and seq![1] are ambiguous integer literals; Verus cannot infer the element type for the sequence. Since ghost_serialize returns Seq<u8>, the sequence literals must be explicitly typed as u8. I fixed this by changing all occurrences of seq![0] to seq![0u8] and seq![1] to seq![1u8] in the Option<T> implementation, including both the ghost_serialize function and the proof lemma.
