Explanation of changes:
- Verus requires that trait method implementations do not restate requires/ensures; they are inherited from the trait. The compilation error arises because the Option<T> implementation redeclared requires/ensures. I removed these from the Option<T> implementation’s method signature.
- In branches where self is None and other is Some (and vice versa), the postcondition must hold only on reachable paths. These branches are impossible due to the tag-byte mismatch implied by equal serializations. I replaced the incorrect assertions of view_equal with explicit contradictions using assert(false) by { ... } to close those unreachable paths soundly.
